Golden She Foundation was established in 2021 by Celia Sibanda.

Our Aim is to Empower Young girls to believe in themselves. There isn't any dream that's too small or too big to be achieved.

being a girl there are certain roles and duties that are expected from us but we forget our dreams. It is possible to archive anything in this lifetime irrespective of your gender.

If we come together as girls/women to empower each other a lot can be achieved. The Foundation priorities young girls as we believe a brighter and better future is ahead of us.
